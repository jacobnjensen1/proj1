Weird Plant Emporium has a bunch of weird plants for sale. These are all sourced legally and responsibly. Unless otherwise noted, all plants for sale were grown in one of our nurseries. WPE is opposed to unlawful and irresponsible plant harvesting, and donates to a charity which helps educate people on the ecological toll that illegal harvesting takes.
